Learning Objectives

This comprehensive training program covers all aspects of industrial maintenance for PLC Fundamentals control systems, including:

  • Understanding PID control principles and system behavior
  • Emergency stop system operation and troubleshooting
  • PLC programming and I/O management
  • HMI interface operation and alarm management
  • Pump and valve control systems
  • Sensor calibration and fault diagnosis
  • Safety procedures and lockout/tagout
  • Preventive maintenance scheduling

Certification Path

Complete all modules and scenarios to earn your Industrial Maintenance Certification:

  • Basic Level: Complete Worksheets 1-7
  • Intermediate Level: Complete Worksheets 8-14
  • Advanced Level: Complete all CP6773 scenarios
